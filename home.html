About

Who We Are

We are a group of young community developers very passionate about literacy, libraries and quality education for every individual

What we Do?

We are putting up libraries in rural primary schools. Libraries create reading, enriching and resourceful heavens, that create space for mental, intellectual, spiritual, creative and innovative growth. Our programme ensures that children read for leisure, fun and growth and gain skills that help them dream, imagine, innovate and develop interests and passions that make them discover and learn more about themselves and their environment.

Mission

Improving literacy levels and education perception among primary school children in rural and marginalised areas In Kenya.

Vision

Driving impact through sustainable changes on key community development, growth and sustainability factors influenced both directly and indirectly by literacy and education perceptions within communities.
